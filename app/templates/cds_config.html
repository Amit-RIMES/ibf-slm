About Copernicus Data Store

SEAS5 — ECMWF's seasonal forecast system, providing 6-month outlooks at ~0.4° resolution. Data is stored in the existing Seasonal Forecasts table as tercile probability estimates.

ERA5-Land — High-resolution (0.1°) reanalysis covering 1950–present. Daily precipitation values are stored in the Observed Rainfall table (source=ERA5) for baseline computations and trigger threshold recommendations.

GloFAS v4 — Global Flood Awareness System providing 10-day ensemble river discharge forecasts at 0.05° resolution for the global river network. Discharge is stored in m³/s.

Large CDS requests are queued and may take minutes to hours. Daily syncs are designed to request small incremental updates. For historical backfills, consider requesting one month at a time.
