About ECMWF Open Data
  • Source: ECMWF Integrated Forecasting System (IFS), freely available at data.ecmwf.int
  • HRES: Single deterministic run, 0.25° resolution, 10-day horizon (steps 24–240h)
  • ENS: 51-member ensemble, 0.5° resolution, 15-day horizon — percentiles (p10/p25/p50/p75/p90) stored for exceedance analysis
  • Parameter: tp — total precipitation accumulation (m, converted to mm)
  • Runs: 00z, 06z, 12z, 18z UTC — 00z available ~08:00 UTC, 12z ~20:00 UTC
  • Triggers: Ingested forecasts are automatically evaluated against all active triggers
  • Lead-time buckets: d1–5 (cumulative at 120h), d6–10 (120h→240h increment)
