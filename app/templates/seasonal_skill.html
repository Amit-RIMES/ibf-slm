Ranked Probability Skill Score (RPSS) measures how much better a forecast is than climatology (equal probability across terciles). RPSS > 0 = skillful; RPSS = 1 = perfect. RPS is computed per forecast from the cumulative probability difference between forecast and observed outcome. Reference: RPSclim = 2/9 ≈ 0.222. Hit rate = fraction of forecasts where the highest-probability tercile matched the observed outcome.
