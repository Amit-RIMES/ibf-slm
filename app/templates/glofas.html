About GloFAS

The Global Flood Awareness System (GloFAS) is operated by the Copernicus Emergency Management Service. It provides river discharge forecasts for the global river network at 0.05° resolution, with a 10-day lead time using an ensemble of 52 members.

Discharge values (m³/s) show where rivers are carrying high volumes of water, which may indicate flood risk. Cells with discharge < 1 m³/s are not shown on the map.

Data source: Copernicus Climate Change Service (C3S) / ECMWF GloFAS v4.0 operational forecasts.
